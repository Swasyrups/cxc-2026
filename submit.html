CxC 2026

Competition
Rulebook

The complete rules and guidelines for CxC 2026. All registered participants are expected to read and agree to these terms before competing.

Last updated: June 2026 Version 1.0
In this document
1. Eligibility 2. Format 3. Recipe Rules 4. Swa Requirement 5. Video Submission 6. Scoring 7. Conduct
Section 01

Eligibility

01

Open to all practising baristas and bartenders across India. No minimum experience required.

02

Stage 1 is an individual competition. You register and submit as a solo entrant — baristas and bartenders compete in separate tracks.

03

Shortlisted individuals from Stage 1 will be paired by the CxC organisers into barista–bartender teams for the regional heats.

04

Employees of Raw Earth Foods & Beverages Pvt. Ltd. and their immediate family members are not eligible.

Section 02

Competition Format

01

Stage 1 — Individual online submission. Video + recipe card. Judged remotely by the CxC panel.

02

Stage 2 — Regional heats in Bangalore, Mumbai, Delhi. Shortlisted teams compete live. Top 3 per city advance.

03

Stage 3 — Grand Finale on 8 September 2026, Bangalore. 15-minute live showcase per team. Full blind judging panel.

Section 03

Recipe Rules

01

One drink submission per person. The recipe must be original and created specifically for CxC 2026.

02

The drink must not have been publicly served at any commercial venue in the 12 months preceding the submission date.

03

All ingredients must be legally purchasable in India. No prohibited substances.

04

Submission deadline is 31 July 2026, 11:59 PM IST. No extensions will be granted under any circumstances.

Section 04

Swa Syrup Requirement

01

At least one Swa Artisanal Syrup must feature as a named ingredient in the final drink. Entries without Swa will be disqualified.

02

You may use multiple Swa syrups. There is no upper limit.

03

The Swa syrup sample kit dispatched on registration must be used in your video demonstration.

Section 05

Video Submission

01

Submit a video between 60 and 90 seconds showing your drink preparation from start to finish.

02

The video must show your face and hands. Voice narration explaining your drink concept is strongly encouraged.

03

Accepted formats: MP4, MOV. Maximum file size: 50MB (expanding shortly). Minimum resolution: 720p.

04

No post-production effects, AI-generated visuals, or footage from commercial shoots. Raw, authentic footage only.

Section 06

Scoring

Stage 1 entries are scored out of 100 points across five criteria.

Craft & Technique25
Flavour & Balance25
Swa Integration20
Creativity & Narrative20
Presentation10
Total100 pts
Section 07

Conduct & Rights

01

Judges' decisions are final and binding. No appeals will be entertained.

02

Any attempt to contact judges directly about your submission will result in immediate disqualification.

03

CxC organisers reserve the right to disqualify any entry that does not meet the spirit of the competition.

04

By submitting, you grant Swa Artisanal Syrups and CxC the non-exclusive right to use your name, photo, video, and recipe content for promotional purposes including social media, paid advertising, and the Swa recipe bank — with full credit given to you as the creator.
